TRAA Would Like to Hear From You

Aside - Resources Info

There's a bunch of ways to get in touch with the TRAA. If you're with a like-minded organization looking for more information on the TRAA and its activities, or an individual who would like to become involved with the activities undertaken by the TRAA, the TRAA would like to hear from you.

Contact – TRAA Meeting Info

TRAA General Meeting

The TRAA meets every second Wednesday of each month. We always try to get the meetings started by 7:00pm so try to be there a few minutes beforehand. The venue is the Western Ontario Fish & Game Protective Association's clubhouse located just east of the Dearness Home on Southdale Road (East), on the North side of Southdale Road. The address is 790 Southdale Road East, London, Ontario. Guests are always welcome!

Note: Every now and then we have "off-site" meetings. For example, the July and August meetings are "fishing meetings" so we won't be at WOFGPA clubhouse. Stay on top of these changes by visiting this website often.
